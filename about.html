SafeTrekr was founded on the belief that every organization—regardless of size or budget— deserves access to professional-grade trip safety management. We're building a platform that makes comprehensive safety analysis fast, affordable, and effortless.

Our Story

Born from real-world frustration

SafeTrekr started in 2023 when two former educators and risk managers realized a fundamental problem: most organizations were either over-spending on complex enterprise risk management systems they didn't need, or under-preparing trips with homemade spreadsheets and crossed fingers.

Our co-founders—Sarah Chen, a former study abroad director who spent countless nights worrying about student safety, and Michael Rodriguez, a risk management consultant tired of $50K implementations for $5K problems—decided there had to be a better way.

They built SafeTrekr to fill the gap: professional analyst review, comprehensive safety documentation, and board-ready trip packets at a price point accessible to schools, churches, and small teams. No enterprise bloat. No per-user fees. Just smart, thorough safety analysis when you need it.

Today, SafeTrekr supports hundreds of organizations planning thousands of trips annually. Every trip is still reviewed by a human safety analyst. Every packet is still customized. And we're still obsessed with making professional safety management radically more accessible.
