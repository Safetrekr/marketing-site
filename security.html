Security & Compliance FAQs

Safetrekr is designed for FERPA compliance. The platform supports organizations that handle student information through role-based access control, data minimization (no SSNs, academic records, or disciplinary records stored), guardian consent tracking, and comprehensive audit trails. Safetrekr does not claim FERPA certification -- no third party certifies FERPA compliance. The platform is designed so that organizations using Safetrekr can meet their own FERPA obligations when managing student trip data. We will sign your standard student data privacy agreement.

Safetrekr is designed for regulatory alignment with FERPA, the Clery Act, Title IX, and GDPR. The platform enforces role-based access control at the database level, encrypts data in transit and at rest, and maintains comprehensive audit trails. We will sign your organization's standard data privacy agreement, including school district DPAs and state-specific student data privacy addenda. See our full Security, Privacy & Compliance Overview for detailed evaluation criteria.

Data is stored on US-based cloud infrastructure. All data is encrypted in transit via TLS and at rest through database-level storage encryption. Participant data is not shared with third parties for marketing, analytics, or any purpose outside the direct delivery of Safetrekr's trip safety management services.

We support right-to-be-forgotten requests per GDPR and similar regulations. When you request data deletion, we permanently remove all personal information within 30 days. Our Evidence Binder system generates cryptographic purge proofs that verify deletion occurred without needing to retain the deleted data. You can configure automatic purge schedules for trip data after retention periods expire.

We have a documented incident response plan with 24/7 monitoring and rapid response procedures. In the event of a security incident, we immediately contain the threat, assess the impact, and notify affected customers within 72 hours per GDPR requirements (or sooner for critical incidents). We provide detailed incident reports and work with your team on any required breach notifications or remediation steps.

Our background check workflows are built to FCRA standards. We ensure proper disclosure and authorization before initiating checks, handle adverse action procedures correctly, and limit access to results based on permissible purposes. Results are encrypted, audit-logged, and automatically purged per retention policies. We integrate with FCRA-compliant screening providers like Checkr and Sterling.
