Data Architecture

Role-based access with database-level isolation

key

Role-Based Access Controls

Access is governed by 10 defined roles, each scoped to specific portal areas and data types. A chaperone cannot see another family's medical disclosures. A trip coordinator sees consent status, not the underlying records. A billing administrator cannot access safety review data.

lock

Database-Level Data Isolation

Safetrekr uses Row Level Security (RLS) enforced at the database layer. Data access restrictions are not enforced solely by application code -- they are enforced by the database itself. An organization's data is isolated from every other organization regardless of what happens at the application layer.

dashboard

Four-Portal Architecture

Safetrekr operates through four separate portals -- Client Portal, Analyst Portal, HQ Console, and Traveler App. Each portal enforces strict boundaries around what data is visible and what actions are available. Users are automatically routed to the appropriate portal based on their assigned role.

encrypted

Data Encryption

All data transmitted between clients and the platform is encrypted in transit via TLS. Data at rest is encrypted through the database provider's storage-level encryption. Authentication tokens are transmitted exclusively over encrypted connections.

history

Comprehensive Audit Trail

Every action is logged from three sources: client actions (trip creation, document uploads, settings changes), analyst actions (review completions, approval decisions), and system events (alert routing, notification delivery, status transitions). Full-text search across all activity with filtering by source, type, and time range.

data_usage

Data Minimization by Design

Safetrekr collects only what is necessary for trip safety management. No Social Security numbers, no academic records, no disciplinary records, no full medical records. Background check status is tracked -- not the underlying report content.

Regulatory Alignment

Designed for your compliance requirements

school

FERPA

Safetrekr is designed for FERPA compliance. The platform supports organizations that handle student information through role-based access control, data minimization (no SSNs, academic records, or disciplinary records stored), guardian consent tracking, and comprehensive audit trails. Safetrekr does not claim FERPA certification -- no third party certifies FERPA compliance. The platform is designed so that organizations using Safetrekr can meet their own FERPA obligations when managing student trip data.

balance

Title IX

Organizations with Title IX obligations can use Safetrekr's documentation and safety review capabilities to support their compliance posture for group travel: background check and screening workflows, documented safety review across 18 dimensions, time-stamped records of every communication and acknowledgment, and board-ready documentation packages.

gavel

Clery Act

For colleges and universities subject to the Clery Act, Safetrekr provides documentation that supports campus safety reporting obligations related to off-campus travel: time-stamped incident and alert records, documented safety reviews with analyst sign-off, communication logs, and searchable audit trails.

public

GDPR

For organizations with international travel involving EU travelers or destinations, Safetrekr's data handling practices support GDPR alignment: right to access, right to data portability, right to erasure (user-initiated deletion with 30-day grace period), timestamped consent management, data minimization, and granular privacy controls.

Background Checks

Background check workflows

Safetrekr integrates with background check providers to track screening status across your organization.

fact_check

Status Tracking

Safetrekr stores check status (approved, pending, not started, rejected, not required, expired) -- not the full background check report. Results are tracked at both the trip level and the organization level. Compliance matrices show clearance status at a glance for every participant and check type.

groups

Multiple Providers

Safetrekr integrates with Checkr, Sterling, and GoodHire. Organizations select their preferred provider or record results from their existing provider directly in the platform. A chaperone cleared this semester does not need to be re-screened next semester if their check is current.

assignment_turned_in

Five Check Types

Criminal background, sex offender registry, driving record, employment verification, and education verification. Domestic checks at $35/person, international checks at $65/person.

Platform infrastructure

US-Based Cloud Infrastructure

US-based cloud infrastructure with containerized deployment, health and readiness monitoring, and separate development and production environments.

Secrets Management

Secrets managed through environment-variable injection at runtime -- not stored in code or build artifacts. Webhook signature verification for inbound communications from third-party services.

security

Authentication

Email and password authentication with secure session management. Two-factor authentication support. Invite-based account activation for organizational portals (no self-registration). Automatic session token refresh for continuity.

delete_forever

Data Retention & Deletion

Intelligence data follows a configurable retention policy with automated cleanup. User accounts can be deleted by the user with a 30-day recovery window before permanent removal. Organizations can request data deletion in accordance with applicable data privacy agreements.
