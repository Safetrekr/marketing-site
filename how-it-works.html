The Process

Five steps from trip idea to defensible record

You plan the trip. A professional analyst reviews it. Your travelers get connected. Conditions are monitored in real time. Everything is documented for the record.

edit_note

1. Plan Your Trip

About 15 minutes

Complete our guided 10-step wizard with everything a safety analyst needs to review your trip: destinations, dates, travelers, flights, lodging, venues, day-by-day itinerary, ground transportation, add-on services, and a final review.

  • Import participants in bulk from CSV or Excel with intelligent matching against your traveler registry
  • Interactive maps visualize every location on your itinerary
  • Lodging entries automatically flag rooms above the sixth floor for fire safety review
  • Flight lookup auto-populates details from a flight number
  • Select add-on services including background checks and screening
person_search

2. Professional Analyst Review

Starting at 3-5 business days

A trained safety analyst independently reviews your trip across 18 safety dimensions. They validate every location, route, and lodging -- then build a structured emergency preparedness plan tailored to your specific trip, destinations, and group composition. Review timelines vary by trip complexity -- domestic day trips in 3-5 business days, multi-day and international trips may require additional time.

  • Nearby hospitals mapped with trauma capability levels and estimated travel times
  • Local emergency services cataloged with direct numbers for every destination
  • Three-tier evacuation plans (shelter-in-place, local evacuation, full relocation) with rally points on interactive maps
  • Safety checklists assigned across eight categories with timing triggers
  • Background check and document compliance reviewed
  • Real-time intelligence assessed for your destinations from 11+ government sources
smartphone

3. Travelers Get Connected

Before departure

Before your group departs, travelers, chaperones, and guardians access the Safetrekr Traveler App -- a mobile-first portal that delivers live safety information directly to their devices.

  • Safety checklists delivered live with timing triggers -- before departure, on arrival, and based on events
  • Emergency preparedness information accessible on-device: contacts, local services, medical facilities, evacuation plans, and rally points
  • Day-by-day itineraries with real-time updates as plans change
  • Documents and consents submitted directly through the app
  • Every acknowledgment tracked -- you know exactly who has reviewed the information and who has not
radar

4. Travel With Confidence

During travel

On travel days, Safetrekr continuously monitors conditions at your destinations from 11+ authoritative government and international sources. When risks are identified, alerts are surfaced for analyst review and routed to affected trips with course-of-action guidance.

  • Monitoring sources include NWS, USGS, CDC, State Department, FEMA, FAA, GDACS, and ReliefWeb
  • Alerts are reviewed by analysts before delivery -- not automated spam
  • Alerts reach your team via email, SMS, and in-app with acknowledgment tracking
  • Automatic escalation for critical events when alerts go unacknowledged
  • Course-of-action guidance tells your team what to do, not just what happened
fact_check

5. Document Everything

After travel, your complete trip record is ready. Every precaution your organization took is documented with timestamps, and board-ready trip packets are generated for every stakeholder who needs them.

  • Every alert sent, with source, severity, delivery channel, and timestamp
  • Every checklist acknowledged, with recipient and acknowledgment time
  • Every analyst review decision documented with evidence and audit trail
  • Board-ready trip packets in four role-based variants: traveler, chaperone, guardian, and administrator
  • Trip packets carry your organization's branding -- logo, colors, watermarks, and custom cover titles
  • A defensible record for your board, insurance provider, and parents
