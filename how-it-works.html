The Process

Four steps from itinerary to safety plan

Upload your trip basics, we handle the verification and planning, you download a complete safety packet.

edit_note

1. Input Your Trip

Upload your itinerary (PDF/CSV/Google Sheet) or fill our guided form with dates, lodging, venues, and headcount. Takes 10-15 minutes. We extract the details and create your trip shell.

  • Trip dates and destination(s)
  • Lodging addresses and venue locations
  • Transportation method and routes
  • Participant count and group type
search

2. Analyst Review

A professional safety analyst reviews your trip, validates all locations, checks lodging safety (egress, floor policy, neighborhood), identifies nearby ER/LE, and provides recommendations. Standard turnaround: 3-5 business days. Rush available.

  • Route validation and fallback options
  • Lodging safety checks and floor guidance
  • Emergency resource proximity scans
  • Risk identification and mitigation advice
picture_as_pdf

3. Custom Trip Packet

Receive a professional PDF trip packet with interactive maps, detailed itineraries, emergency contacts, safety protocols, compliance documentation, and audit logs. Available as PDF and mobile-friendly HTML.

  • Maps with routes, lodging, and ER/LE locations
  • Lodging and venue safety checklists
  • Emergency call tree and contact cards
  • Time-stamped audit logs and purge proofs
download

4. Download & Travel

Download your packet, share with board members and stakeholders, and travel with confidence. Optional add-ons: background checks (with consent), parent communications kit, incident debrief, and translation packs.

  • PDF and mobile-friendly HTML formats
  • Day-of AM/PM brief emails
  • Export options for mass-notify systems
  • Optional background checks and comms kits

Live Updates

Day-of AM/PM briefs keep you informed

Concise, actionable updates delivered to trip leads every morning and evening during your trip.

What's in a Brief

  • Morning Plan: Weather, events near venues, transit advisories, any overnight changes
  • Evening Review: Next-day outlook, route conditions, local event notices
  • Alerts: Urgent changes with proposed actions (reroute, hotel floor change, etc.)
  • Read Confirmation: Simple one-tap "I read it" with roll-call for chaperones

Privacy by design: Only designated leads receive updates. Everything is logged for accountability.

FAQ

Common questions about the process

How much prep do we need before submitting? expand_more

A basic itinerary is enough—dates, destinations, lodging, and venues. We'll ask for anything missing. You can upload a PDF, CSV, or Google Sheet, or fill our guided form.

What if we change hotels or add a stop after submission? expand_more

Re-upload or edit in your dashboard. We'll update the binder and notify your trip leads. Rush fees may apply for short-notice changes close to departure.

Do you cover international trips? expand_more

Yes. Timelines may be longer depending on location data availability. We include country intel in AM/PM briefs, embassy/consulate info, local ER/LE contacts, and visa/passport checklists. We confirm specifics at intake.

Who sees our traveler information? expand_more

Only your designated admins/leads and SafeTrekr analysts with role-based access. Background check results are further restricted to authorized admins only. We never share data with third parties.

What if the internet is down during our trip? expand_more

Your binder is downloadable as a PDF. The mobile HTML view works offline once loaded (PWA). We recommend downloading the PDF before departure as a backup.

Do you store full medical records? expand_more

No. We only store high-level flags if you provide them (e.g., "allergy flag"). Full medical records remain with your organization. We practice data minimization by design.

Can we see a sample binder before committing? expand_more

Yes. We provide redacted sample binders for different trip types. Request a sample binder here.

What file formats do you accept for itineraries? expand_more

PDF, CSV, Excel, Google Sheets, or paste directly into our form. We extract dates, locations, and contacts automatically. If we can't parse something, we'll ask you to clarify.

Are background checks required for all trips? expand_more

No. Background checks are an optional add-on. Add them when your policy requires screening, for overnight trips with youth, or when venues mandate them. Adult consent is always required before screening.

How long do you retain our trip data? expand_more

We follow your retention policy. Standard: audit logs for 7 years (compliance), trip details purged on schedule (typically 90 days post-trip). You receive cryptographic purge-proofs as evidence of deletion.
