How It Works

From trip idea to defensible record in 5 steps

edit_note

1. Plan Your Trip

Complete our guided 10-step wizard in 10-15 minutes. Destinations, dates, travelers, flights, lodging, venues, itinerary, and transportation -- with interactive maps and bulk participant import.

person_search

2. Professional Analyst Review

A safety analyst validates every location across 18 dimensions. They build your emergency preparedness plan, map nearby hospitals with trauma levels, and assess real-time intelligence.

smartphone

3. Travelers Get Connected

Travelers, chaperones, and guardians access the Traveler App. Safety checklists are delivered live. Emergency information is on-device. Documents and consents can be submitted directly.

radar

4. Travel With Confidence

During travel, Safetrekr continuously monitors conditions at your destinations. Alerts are reviewed by an analyst and routed to your team with acknowledgment tracking and escalation.

fact_check

5. Document Everything

After travel, your complete record is ready -- every alert sent, every checklist acknowledged, every safety decision documented. Board-ready packets for your board, insurer, and parents.
